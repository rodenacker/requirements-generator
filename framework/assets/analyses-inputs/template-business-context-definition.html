    How the four artefacts connect: each Business Problem (or opportunity) creates a Business Need, which a Business Goal / Objective resolves, which a human-centered Problem Statement reframes for solution exploration. Inferred nodes (amber) are anchored and auditable. Opportunity-driven chains correctly have no problem.
