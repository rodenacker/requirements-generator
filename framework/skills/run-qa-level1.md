# run-qa-level1.md

**Purpose:** Level 1 Q&A — individual, focused questions on flagged items in the spec. One sharp question at a time; natural-language answers accepted; no batched suggestions.

**Inputs:** flagged items from `flag-gaps-ambiguities.md` or completeness-report findings, current spec, the Q&A character (`characters/requirements-qa.md` or `characters/design-qa.md`).

**Outputs:** consultant answers captured for `reconcile.md` to fold back in.

**Used by:**
- `framework/agents/requirements-qa/agent.md` (Level 1 internal phase).
- `framework/agents/design-qa/agent.md` (Level 1 internal phase).

**Used how:** Calibrated to the consultant audience (calibration baked in at design time per `plan/audience-profile-consultant.md`; never runtime-loaded); short numbered lists with natural-language shortcuts over walls of suggestion prose.
